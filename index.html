Premier HVAC Contractor Serving San Francisco

San Francisco Heating & Air Conditioning Services is a locally owned and operated HVAC company serving the entire San Francisco Bay Area from our base at 690 Andover St, San Francisco, CA 94110. Our team brings years of hands-on experience in the HVAC industry, delivering expert solutions for both residential and commercial clients.

San Francisco's unique microclimate — with its cool, foggy summers and chilly, wet winters — places extraordinary demands on home heating and cooling systems. From the historic Victorians of Noe Valley and the Mission District to modern condos in SoMa and the Marina, every building requires tailored HVAC solutions. Our technicians understand the nuances of San Francisco's diverse architecture and can recommend and install the perfect system for your space.

Whether you need an emergency furnace repair in the middle of a cold snap, a central air conditioning installation to prepare for warmer months, or routine HVAC maintenance to keep your system running efficiently, we are here to help. We work with all major brands and types of equipment, including gas furnaces, electric heat pumps, ductless mini-splits, packaged rooftop units, and smart thermostats.

As a Google-rated 4.8-star business with over 30 satisfied reviews, we take pride in our reputation for honesty, professionalism, and quality craftsmanship. We offer same-day service for urgent repairs, free estimates on new installations, and upfront pricing so you always know what to expect. From Bernal Heights to Pacific Heights, the Sunset to the Richmond, call us today and experience the difference that real HVAC expertise makes.
